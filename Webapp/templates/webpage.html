Counting Colonies in Bacterial Cultures

By Aurélien Bück-Kaeffer, Anne-Claudèle Fortin-Hoang, Victor Cruz


About this project

Given a picture of a bacterial culture, this program aims at counting the bacterial colonies to ouput a range that is representative of the number of colonies present on the sample.


Let's count bacteries!
